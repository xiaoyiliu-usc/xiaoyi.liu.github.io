About Me

Hello! I am Liu Xiaoyi, an assistant professor at the School of Traditional Chinese Medicine at Beijing University of Chinese Medicine. I received my Ph.D. in Engineering from the University of South Carolina in 2024. My research focuses on integrating AI with Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) to improve manufacturing and quality control processes. My work aims to bridge the gap between artificial intelligence and TCM through interdisciplinary research.

Research Areas:

  • AI-driven intelligent pharmaceutical manufacturing systems
  • Machine learning algorithms for TCM manufacturing processes
  • Generative AI for drug discovery
  • Multi-modal knowledge graphs for TCM

Research Projects

  • NSF Project: AI-driven composite materials manufacturing system (2021-2024)
  • National Key R&D Program: Network models for industrial biomanufacturing (2022-2026)
  • National Natural Science Foundation: Smart recognition of drug molecules (2022-2025)
