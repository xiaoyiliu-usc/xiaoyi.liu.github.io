About Me

Hello! I am Xiaoyi Liu, an assistant professor at the School of Chinese Meteria Medica, Beijing University of Chinese Medicine. I received my Ph.D. in Engineering from the University of South Carolina in 2024.

Research Areas:

  • Generative AI for drug discovery
  • Retrosynthesis
  • Smart manufacturing
  • Large Language Model
  • Machine learning driven TCM manufacturing processes
