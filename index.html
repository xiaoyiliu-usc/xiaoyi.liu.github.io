About Me

I am a Lecturer at the School of Chinese Materia Medica, Beijing University of Chinese Medicine, and a native of Pinggu, Beijing. I obtained my Ph.D. in Engineering from the University of South Carolina in 2024. My interdisciplinary research focuses on leveraging artificial intelligence and traditional Chinese medicine information science to enhance intelligent manufacturing processes and quality control.

My research also spans AI-driven industrial intelligence and synthetic biology, aiming to accelerate innovative drug development and create generative AI solutions for scientific research.
